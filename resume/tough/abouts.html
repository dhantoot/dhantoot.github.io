Company History

In March 2013, the IRA-AIR Innovative Research & Analysis, Inc. has been established. It first established itself as a trading and R&D company for renewable energy and dealt with consulting services to different clients in its line of business and more. IRA-AIR focuses its trading/R&D on eco-friendly products and has established its position as the leading company in developing for a clean and environmentally-friendly society. IRA-AIR’s philosophy lies in ensuring the responsibility for the society that it resides in and believes in everyone’s role and importance of governance in taking care of this world. From Trading to R&D on the LED lights, Solar Energy- Renewable Energy and Energy Management System, all of IRA-AIR’s business focuses on developing the quality of life for all people. Its vision and mission promises all that a responsible company should do and every member of the organization has embraced the company’s responsibilities.
